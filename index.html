How the Content Mix Model™ Works

From Pixel to Transaction: The Deterministic Spine

01 Fingerprint

AI decomposes every ad into 20+ metadata tags (Mood, Talent, Product, Call to Action).

02 Expose

We capture device-level IDs via cookieless S2S tracking across all digital and audio channels.

03 Match

DeliverAI reconciles exposures against your transaction data in a zero-PII, privacy-safe vault.

04 Optimize

Daily recommendations on which creative elements are over-performing in real-time.
