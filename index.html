Framework

How It Works

Step 1Content ID

Assigns a persistent unique identifier to each creative asset, enabling tracking and analysis across channels.

Step 2Exposure

Tracks the exposure of content using a pixel/server-to-server/clean room to capture the device IDs.

Step 3Identity Match

Matches the exposure data to individual user identities.

Step 4Attribution

Proprietary ML assigns attribution of outcomes to the specific content that drove the desired actions.

Step 5Outcome

Measures outcomes such as site visits, lift, and conversions associated with the content.

Step 6Optimization

Proprietary ML scores and ranks content performance, providing media buy recommendations on what to scale, repackage, or drop.
